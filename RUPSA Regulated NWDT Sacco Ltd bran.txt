## 1. Color Palette & Typography

* **Primary Corporate Color:** A vibrant **lime green / leaf green** used for primary text headers, background banners, and accent design lines.
* **Secondary Corporate Color:** **Solid Black** for secondary text, the main "SACCO" wordmark, and body copy accents.

Occasional decorative brush/script fonts are reserved purely for celebratory or thematic titles like "Father's Day" in `image_417f3b.png`.

* **Text Layout:** The word **RUPSA** is rendered in bold lime green sans-serif directly underneath the hands, sitting above the word **—SACCO—**, which is written in solid black sandwiched between two thin horizontal black lines.
